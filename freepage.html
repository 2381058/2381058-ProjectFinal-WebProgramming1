Philippine Madrigal Singers

History of Philippine Madrigal Singers

The University of the Philippines Madrigal Singers was organized in 1963 by National Artist Professor Andrea O. Veneracion. The group is composed of students, faculty and alumni from the different colleges of the University of the Philippines (UP). The choir is one of the world’s most awarded, having consistently won all the top prizes in most of the world’s prestigious choral competitions.

Why I Love Philippine Madrigal Singers

Philippine Madrigal Singers have a different sound from other choirs, philippine madrigal or what can be abbreviated as philmadz, has a beautiful sound, when you hear it, it feels like being in heaven. With not many members, compared to other choirs, but they can make the sound as if it was produced by dozens of people then with a sitting singing position, that's what makes me really like them.
